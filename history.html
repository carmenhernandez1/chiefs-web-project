Arrowhead was yet to be built yet, but it was in the works. The goal of the stadium was to provide the fans and players the most home field advantage in NFL history. With over 75,000 seats in this stadium this was meant to be the biggest stadium in the NFL. Fastfoward, about 50 years later, little did we know t would become the loudest stadium in the NFL.
